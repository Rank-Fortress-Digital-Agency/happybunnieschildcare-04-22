Babies in Spring

Hi, Happy Bunnies parents and friends!

Last week our babies were exploring apples. It was great to see them taste, smell, and play with apples. We also made cool art, developing fine motor skills and imagination.

This week our babies are moving onto the Spring theme. We will be making some spring sensory bottles, we will read stories about birds and listen to birds singing. This is a great activity where babies are getting ready for the first stages of phonics.

We will wrap up our week with a concert of spring songs. I am sure our babies will love what we prepared for them this week!
